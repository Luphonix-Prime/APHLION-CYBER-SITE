What is a Security Operations Center?

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized facility that houses an information security team responsible for monitoring, detecting, analyzing, and responding to cybersecurity incidents on an ongoing basis. Our SOC combines advanced technology, skilled analysts, and proven processes to provide comprehensive security monitoring and rapid incident response.
