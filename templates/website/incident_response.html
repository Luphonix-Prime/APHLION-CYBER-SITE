What is Incident Response?

Incident Response is a structured methodology for handling and managing security breaches or cyberattacks. Our comprehensive approach ensures rapid detection, containment, eradication, and recovery from security incidents while minimizing business impact.

Our Proven IR Process

Following industry-standard NIST and SANS frameworks, our incident response process ensures systematic and effective handling of security incidents.

1. Detection & Analysis

Rapid identification and initial assessment of security incidents through advanced monitoring and threat intelligence.

  • • 24/7 Security Monitoring
  • • Threat Intelligence Analysis
  • • Incident Classification
  • • Impact Assessment

2. Containment

Immediate actions to prevent incident spread while preserving evidence for forensic analysis.

  • • System Quarantine
  • • Evidence Preservation

3. Eradication

Complete removal of threats and vulnerabilities that led to the security incident.
